-UPS Shipping Method for WooCommerce-

-Installation-
1- Upload the plugin folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2- Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

-Usage-
1- Once activated, go to WooCommerce > Settings > Shipping Methods
2- Choose UPS from the list at the top of the screen
3- Configure the shipping method with your credentials.
